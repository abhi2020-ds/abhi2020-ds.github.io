The idea behind this development pod space is for myself and fellow readers to find data engineering and science based technical resources and conceptual blogs highlighting every design aspect of a data science project. This will help increase domain on this space for myself and readers of this pod space.

Hoping that content shared here would help in contributing to new ideas. Through blogs highlighting what I have learnt during my journey on exploring data science and BI engineering platform, my goal is to expand my technical domain in this space and help others along the way.

What does the visualization on top reminds you of? To me it reflects my perception of data around us. As a data enthusiast I see it everwhere, as we live in this fast paced digital world we all are contributing to either creation, manipulation, or distribution of new or data. We are processing millions of information everyday, it is logical to understand the technologies behind realm of data science. This curiousity will help create logical patterns and scenarios that will answer critical or complex questions we would have in our mind.

Regardless of what you choose as the technology platform , they all have similar objectives and goals, to explore, to analyze to compute and to answer any question one might have. Engineering and Science go in pair, it is not hard to figure that out.

My objective here is to share my learning, ideas, through concepts and program codes and grow my technical skills down the line. With this excitement , I welcome you all to my dev pod!
