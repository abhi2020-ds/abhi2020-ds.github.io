Dev Pod

Success cannot be achieved without encountering failures along the way - Eat Code Sleep Repeat

Idea behind development pod space is to explore and learn data engineering and science based technical resources and concepts, to understand different development and design aspects in field of data science. Ultimately, to apply what is learnt into real world use cases. With assumption that information shared would help in contributing to new ideas.

What does the visualization on top reminds you of? For me this viz reflects perception of data around us. As a data enthusiast I see it everywhere, as we live in a fast paced digital world we all are contributing to either creation, manipulation, or distribution of data. Processing millions of information everyday through standard digital media and IoTs, thus, it is natural to analyze technologies behind realm of data science. Such curiousity usually results in identifying logical patterns and scenarios that will answer critical and complex questions about data we consume. Nothing tops drafting analysis on any topic by utilizing facts obtained by consuming huge volume of data.

Choose any language or technology for data engineering, they all have similar goals, to explore, to analyze, to compute structutred and unstructured information. Engineering and Science go in pair, that is not hard to figure out.

Through Dev pod I will share my experience, and ideas around data science concepts and supporting technologies. I hope this effort towards creating a one stop workspace for all will help data science enthusiasts like myself. In return, also, hopefully blogging will help me enhance my technical and authoring skills. With enthusiam , this passionate data enthusiast welcomes you all ! :)
